About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, where I am advised by Prof. Zhaozheng Yin. I received my M.S. in Computer Science from George Washington University and my B.S. in Automation from Northeastern University in China. My research interests lie in multimodal deep learning, language-based video retrieval, temporal sentence grounding, and multimodal LLMs.
